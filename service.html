Consultancy

At Thomas & Partners, we take complex business challenges, simplify them and convert them into real business value. We leave no stone unturned to find potential, even in the highest performing of travel programmes. We deliver travel solutions that support your business as you innovate, expand and enter new markets.

Extracting the very best from an airline programme requires a comprehensive understanding of macro-economic market conditions, insight into industry trends and an awareness of how these might impact your business. We leave basic fare comparison behind and dig deeper into the complex world of air travel to help you maximise your travel solutions.

Businesses are expanding internationally, open booking is gaining in popularity and social buying is creating a state of flux in the market. Companies typically underestimate their total spend and the time they need to commit to achieve better value. We capture and analyse your data against defined objectives and benchmark these against your peers, identifying gaps and highlighting opportunities to drive savings and policy adoption.
